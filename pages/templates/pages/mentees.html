Becoming a Mentee



CenterShip mentees are young BIPOC high school girls who have interests in STEM. Our mentees come from a wide variety of backgrounds, but they all share a passion for growing and learning within a community.

What to Expect:
1. You will be able to choose a mentor based on your interests in a STEM topic, field, or even career. Such as, Computer Science, Maths, Data Science, Engineering, etc.

2. Agree and commit to the expectations and goals you set with your mentor.

3. Be receptive to suggestions and feedback from you mentor. Criticisms will only be offered in an effort to help you grow professionally.

4. Keep your goals and those of your mentor in mind during every interaction.
